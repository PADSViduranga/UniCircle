UniCircle

Connecting Campus, One Circle at a Time

Our Mission

UniCircle is a comprehensive university management platform designed to centralize student activities. Our goal is to provide a single space where students can manage their Clubs, participate in Events, and contribute to Charity campaigns without the hassle of multiple fragmented systems.


What We Do

Feature Description
Club Management Tools for Presidents and Vice-Presidents to manage memberships and approvals.
Event Coordination A centralized calendar for all university events with easy registration.
Charity & Volunteering Points-based system to encourage students to give back to the community.

The Development Team

  • Backend Architecture: Spring Boot, Spring Security, and JPA.
  • Frontend Integration: Thymeleaf Template Engine and HTML5.
  • Database Design: Relational data mapping for Users, Clubs, and Events.
